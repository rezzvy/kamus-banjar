What is banjar language?

The Banjar or Banjarese (basa Banjar) is an Austronesian language predominantly spoken by the Banjarese—an indigenous ethnic group native to the Banjar regions—in southeastern Kalimantan, Indonesia. The Banjarese language serves as the de facto lingua franca for various indigenous communities, especially in South Kalimantan. Additionally, it is widely used in Central Kalimantan, and in East Kalimantan overall.

How this translator works?

In general, this application is a web-based application that will translate Indonesian language into Banjar language. The workflow mechanism involves breaking down the existing sentences into several words, and each separated word will be replaced according to the available dataset.
